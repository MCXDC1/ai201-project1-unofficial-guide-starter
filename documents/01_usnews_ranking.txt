University of Connecticut is ranked No. 58 (tie) out of 194 in Best Engineering Schools
The School of Engineering at University of Connecticut has a rolling application deadline. The application fee is $75 for U.S. residents and $75 for international students. Its tuition is full-time: $19,512 per year (in-state); full-time: $41,424 per year (out-of-state); part-time: $1,084 per credit (in-state); and part-time: $2,302 per credit (out-of-state). The 2025 Ph.D. student-faculty ratio is 3.3:1. The School of Engineering at University of Connecticut has 162 full-time faculty on staff.
